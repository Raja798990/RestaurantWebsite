Restaurant Platform

One-liner

A smart restaurant web application where customers can log in and reserve tables online within fixed hours, while the owner manages bookings, uploads menus, and communicates automatically through email and WhatsApp notifications.

Problem & Target Audience

Many restaurants still rely on manual or phone-based reservations, which causes confusion, double bookings, and missed opportunities. This platform introduces a streamlined reservation system with automated communication and analytics. It targets small and medium-sized restaurant owners who want a professional online presence and customers who prefer digital booking.

Insurance Risk Calculator Platform

One-liner

A web-based insurance platform that calculates personalized risk scores for clients based on age, income, health habits, and other data, helping insurers determine fair premiums and risk categories.

Problem & Target Audience

Insurance companies often rely on manual assessments and outdated tools for risk analysis, leading to inefficiencies and human bias. This project automates risk evaluation through a modern web dashboard where users can enter their data and receive instant insights. It targets both insurance professionals and clients looking for transparency in risk assessment.

üë§ User Side (Client Portal)
